# Skillsurger - AI Career Agent Platform

## Overview
Skillsurger is an AI-powered career development platform designed to help job seekers and professionals optimize their careers through intelligent tools and personalized guidance.

## Core Features
- AI Resume Builder: Create ATS-optimized resumes with AI-powered suggestions
- Mock Interviews: Practice interviews with AI feedback
- Job Matching: Smart job discovery based on skills and goals
- Career Development: Personalized learning paths and upskilling recommendations
- CV Score Analysis: Get detailed feedback on resume quality
- AI Mentor Chat: 24/7 AI career guidance

## Primary Use Cases
1. Job seekers looking to optimize their resumes for ATS systems
2. Professionals seeking career advancement and upskilling
3. Recent graduates exploring career paths
4. Career changers pivoting to new industries
5. Interview preparation and practice

## Target Audience
- Job seekers across all experience levels
- Students and recent graduates
- Career changers and professionals
- Tech and non-tech professionals in India and globally
